Find the right plan for you

Basic Welness

Fitness: Access to a limited set of basic workout routines (e.g., 3-4 simple workouts per week). Nutrition: General nutrition tips and a sample meal plan. Mental Wellness: Weekly mindfulness exercises and basic stress-relief tips. Progress Tracking: Limited tracking with basic metrics (e.g., weight, steps). Support: Community support forum and access to general content.

Fit & balanced

Personalized workout plans with 5-6 workouts per week, including strength, cardio, and flexibility exercises. Nutrition: Custom meal plans based on dietary preferences and goals (e.g., weight loss, muscle gain). Mental Wellness: Guided meditation and stress management strategies, including access to weekly mental wellness content. Progress Tracking: Detailed tracking with personalized insights and weekly progress reports. Support: Access to email support for fitness, nutrition, and wellness questions. Extras: Access to fitness challenges and monthly wellness tips.

Advanced Welness

Fully customized workout plans with daily routines tailored to your needs, plus access to exclusive workout videos. Advanced meal plans that adapt to changing goals and preferences, plus personalized macro and micro-nutrient tracking. Exclusive access to guided therapy sessions, personalized mental health plans, and weekly 1-on-1 check-ins with a wellness coach. Full integration with wearable devices for real-time fitness and health data, and advanced tracking features with custom milestones. Priority support with 24/7 chat and phone access to a dedicated coach or specialist. Extras: VIP access to exclusive events, workshops, and wellness webinars. Monthly consultations with a fitness trainer and a nutritionist.
